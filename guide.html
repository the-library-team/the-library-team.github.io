DrakeNieR:

Full Guide

This guide is meant to be a comprehensive list and guide for all media in the Drakengard/Drag-On Dragoon and NieR franchises in release order.

This guide is for those lore buffs who want to experience everything in these franchises, in order to fully engross themselves in the stories and understand the lore.

For normal people, all you will need to experience is the games and if interested, possibly some of the more notable side-content. This is not for you. This guide will contain almost every piece of content, no matter how niche. This includes the games, novels, novellas, manga, anime, stage plays, and more.


(This guide is formatted into 6 sections, separated by the major game releases. Each section will begin with a game and will contain all content released until the next game. Since this guide is in release order however, this means that some content relating to specific games may fall under the label of an unrelated game. Because of this, at the end of each section will be a "related content" section that will link to any content that may fall in other sections.)

(All release dates shown will be the earliest date. This will usually be the Japanese release but will not always be)
