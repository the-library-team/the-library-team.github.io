Welcome to

The Library

The Library is a preservation of novels and novellas relating to the Drakengard/Drag-on Dragoon and NieR franchises. The Library strives to maintain the most complete, clear, and accurate collection of these pieces of media. The Library formats, organizes, and minorly edits these novels and novellas in terms of grammar and formatting for the best reader experience.

The Library takes no credit for any translation or content. All credit and compliments should go to those who have originally created, published, and translated these pieces for the community. Any content that has been translated by fans will be credited to the best of our abilities. Thank you for all of your translations and efforts towards the community.
